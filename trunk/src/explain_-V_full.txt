.TP
GMT_V_OPT
Selects verbose mode, which will send progress reports to IT(stderr).
Choose among 5 levels of verbosity; each level adds mode messages:
.br
BD(0) - Complete silence, not even fatal error messages are produced.
.br
BD(1) - Produce only fatal error messages (same as when OPT(V) is omitted).
.br
BD(2) - Produce also warnings and progress messages (same as OPT(V) only).
.br
BD(3) - Produce also detailed progress messages.
.br
BD(4) - Produce also debugging messages.
