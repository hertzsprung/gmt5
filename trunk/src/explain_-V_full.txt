.TP
GMT_V_OPT
Select verbose mode, which will send progress reports to IT(stderr).
Choose among 6 levels of verbosity; each level adds mode messages:
.br
BD(q) - Complete silence, not even fatal error messages are produced.
.br
BD(f) - Produce only fatal error messages.
.br
BD(c) - Produce also compatibility warnings (same as when OPT(V) is omitted).
.br
BD(n) - Produce also warnings and progress messages (same as OPT(V) only).
.br
BD(v) - Produce also detailed progress messages.
.br
BD(d) - Produce also debugging messages.
