.TP
GMT_SCALE
Draws a simple map scale centered on IT(lon0/lat0).  Use OPT(Lx) to specify x/y position instead.
Scale is calculated at latitude IT(slat) (optionally supply longitude IT(slon) for oblique projections
[Default is central meridian]), IT(length) is in km, or append unit from BD(e)|BD(f)|BD(k)|BD(M)|BD(n)|BD(u).
Use OPT(Lf) to get a "fancy" scale [Default is plain]. Append BD(+l) to
select the default label which equals the distance unit (meter, foot, km, mile, nautical mile, US survey foot) and is justified on top of the scale [t].
Change this by giving your own label (append BD(+l)IT(label)).  Change label justification with BD(+j)IT(justification)
(choose among l(eft), r(ight), t(op), and b(ottom)).  Apply BD(+u) to append the unit to all distance annotations along the scale. 
If you want to place a rectangle behind the scale, specify suitable BD(+p)IT(pen) and/or BD(+f)IT(fill) parameters.
