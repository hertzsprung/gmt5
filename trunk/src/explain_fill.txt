.SS SPECIFYING FILL
.TP
IT(fill)
The attribute IT(fill) specifies the solid shade or solid IT(color)
(see SPECIFYING COLOR below) or the pattern used for filling polygons.
Patterns are specified as BD(p)IT(dpi/pattern), where IT(pattern) gives
the number of the built-in pattern (1-90) IT(or) the name of a Sun 1-, 8-,
or 24-bit raster file. The IT(dpi) sets the resolution of the image. For
1-bit rasters: use BD(P)IT(dpi/pattern) for inverse video, or append
BD(:F)IT(color)[BD(B)[IT(color)]] to specify fore- and background
colors (use IT(color) = - for transparency).
See BD(GMT) Cookbook & Technical Reference Appendix E for information
on individual patterns.
