GMT Mailing Lists

Two electronic mailing lists are available for users to subscribe to.
  1. gmt-help@hawaii.edu provides a forum for GMT users to exchange ideas and ask questions about GMT usage, installation, portability, etc. Please use this utility rather than sending questions directly to us; you are much more likely to get a speedy reply.
  2. gmt-group@hawaii.edu is a one-way street for us to notify users when a new version has been released. No user can post to this list. Please subscribe to this list if you ONLY want email when a new version is available.
To add yourself to these mailinglist (maintained by the listproc program), send email to listproc@hawaii.edu with a one-line message. E.g., if your name is John Smith then send

subscribe gmt-help John Smith

Listproc automatically obtains your email address. To unsubscribe, send

unsubscribe gmt-help

instead. The same procedure is used for gmt-group.

To browse previously posted messages, Stephan Eickschen (eickschen.haenel@t-online.de) maintains a GMT Mailing List Archive. gmt-help is also available as a readonly netnews group via NNTP as gis.gmtlist at server news2.sinica.edu.tw.

Occasionally, a frustrated new GMT user will convince him/herself that their work is so important that they are justified in digging up our phone numbers and calling us directly to get personal attention to their problem. Please don't even think about doing this unless you are prepared to pay our standard consulting fee of USD 200 per hour...
