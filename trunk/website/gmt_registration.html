Location Map:

If you supply us with the longitude and latitude of you work-place we will automatically add a circle to the GMT usage map on the GMT home page (This is done once a day only). You can use the format of your choice. These longitudes are all eqivivalent: 70.7W, -70.7, 70:42 W, -70:42.
Same goes for latitudes: 33.333 S, -33.333, 33:20 S, -33:20. You can also add :ss for arc seconds,
e.g., 33:20:15W

Online Help and Information:


Two electronic mailing lists are available for users to subscribe to. The first is called gmt-group@hawaii.edu and is primarily a 1-way street for us to notify the users when bugs have been fixed or when new updates have been installed in the ftp directory.
We also maintain another list (gmt-help@hawaii.edu) which interested users may subscribe to. It basically provides a forum for GMT users to exchange ideas and ask questions about GMT usage, installation and portability, etc. Please use this utility rather than sending questions directly to us. We hope you appreciate that we simply do not have time to be everybody's personal GMT tutor.
